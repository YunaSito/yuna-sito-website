About

Hi, I'm Yuna Sito.

I believe every property decision is one of the biggest financial commitments you'll make, and having the right guidance can make all the difference. Whether you're buying, selling, investing, or renting, my goal is to help you make informed decisions with confidence while ensuring your property journey is smooth, strategic, and stress-free.

Previously, I worked as a full-time Quality Engineer before taking a leap of faith to pursue my passion as a real estate consultant in Singapore. Today, I'm a property consultant with ERA Singapore, currently with Preeminent Group, the No. 1 group in ERA Singapore.

I believe every successful transaction comes down to careful planning, honest advice, effective marketing, and strong negotiation. Every client has different goals, and I'm committed to providing a strategy that's tailored to achieve the best possible outcome.
